About me

I'm Mahesh Babu and I'm a

Ghattamaneni Mahesh Babu was born on 9 August 1975 in a Telugu speaking family in Madras (now Chennai), Tamil Nadu, India.[11] He is the fourth of the five children of Telugu actor Krishna and Indira, after Ramesh Babu, Padmavathi, and Manjula and before Priyadarshini. Their family hails from Burripalem in Guntur district, Andhra Pradesh.[12] Babu spent his childhood mostly in Madras under the care of his maternal grandmother Durgamma and the rest of his family.[13] He was educated at St. Bede's Anglo Indian Higher Secondary School, Chennai.[14] He obtained a bachelor's degree in commerce from the Loyola College, Chennai.[15] He stated, he was unable to read and write Telugu and used to memorise the dialogues given by his directors during the dubbing phase of his films.

Prabhas

Uppalapati Venkata Suryanarayana Prabhas Raju, known mononymously as Prabhas, is an Indian actor who works predominantly in Telugu cinema. One of the highest-paid actors in Indian cinema, Prabhas has featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list three times since 2015 based on his income and popularity.

Ram Charan

Konidela Ram Charan Teja is an Indian actor, producer, and entrepreneur who primarily works in Telugu films. One of the highest-paid actors in Indian cinema, he is the recipient of three Filmfare Awards and two Nandi Awards. Since 2013, he has featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list.

Allu Arjun

Allu Arjun is an Indian actor who works in Telugu films. One of the highest paid actors in India, Arjun is also known for his extraordinary dancing skills. He is a recipient of several awards including six Filmfare Awards and three Nandi Awards. Allu Arjun made his debut with Gangotri in 2003.
